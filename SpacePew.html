Space Pew

Description

In this project I learned how to apply post processing effect such as bloom and color grading, I also took the initiative to make a difficulty system in which enemy bullet stats (and their appearance) change depending on your score. Furthermore, I made a bullet effect system where you can add as many modifiers to bullet stats as you want.
